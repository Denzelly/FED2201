My name is Denzel! I am a physicist, data analyst, musician, and much more. I'm currently a graduate student at Georgia State University where I've been investigating a 2-Channel Pot Detector, and subsequently the muon & neutron events we detect from it. I love research and have been conducting projects since my freshman year of undergrad. This is where my coding career began. Before moving on to other endeavors, I was working to create an application structured around FitBit's OAuth 2.0 framework that would allow my colleagues and I access to the real-time physiological data provided by the FitBit users who'd grant us permission. I enjoy learning new things and hope that one day I'll have the pleasure of helping others in their pursuit of similar goals.
