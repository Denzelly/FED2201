My name is Denzel and I am a physicist as well as a musician. I'm currently a graduate student at Georgia State University where I've been investigating a 2-Channel Pot Detector, and subsequently the muon & neutron events we detect from it. I love research and have been since my freshman year of undergrad. I enjoy learning new things and hope that one day I'll have the pleasure of helping others in their pursuit of similar goals.
